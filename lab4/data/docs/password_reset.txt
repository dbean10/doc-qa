Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password, click "Forgot password?" on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account and we will send you a reset link.

The reset link is valid for 1 hour. If it expires, return to the login page and request a new one.

When choosing a new password, use at least 12 characters including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and at least one symbol. Avoid using personal information such as your name or birthdate.

If you have access to your account but want to change your password, go to Settings > Security > Password. You will need to enter your current password before setting a new one.

For accounts protected by single sign-on (SSO), password changes must be made through your identity provider, not through this platform.
