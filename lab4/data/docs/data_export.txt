Exporting Your Data

You can export all data associated with your account at any time from Settings > Data > Export.

Supported export formats are JSON and CSV. JSON exports include full metadata and nested relationships. CSV exports flatten the data and are best suited for spreadsheet analysis.

Large exports are processed asynchronously. After requesting an export, you will receive an email with a download link when the file is ready. Typical processing time is under 10 minutes for accounts with under 10,000 records.

Export files are available for download for 7 days. After that they are deleted from our servers.

If you need to export data on a recurring schedule, use the API's /export endpoint which supports automated programmatic access to the same data.
