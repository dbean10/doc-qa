Contacting Support

Support is available through several channels depending on your plan.

Free plan: Community forum and documentation only.
Starter plan: Email support with a response time of 2 business days.
Professional plan: Email support with a response time of 8 business hours.
Enterprise plan: Dedicated Slack channel with a response time of 1 hour, plus a named account manager.

To open a support ticket, click "Help" in the bottom-left corner of the interface and select "Contact Support." Describe your issue in detail including steps to reproduce any problems, and attach screenshots if relevant.

For urgent issues on Professional or Enterprise plans, mark your ticket as "High Priority." This escalates the ticket to the on-call support engineer.

The community forum at community.example.com is publicly accessible and contains answers to frequently asked questions and tips from other users.
