Custom Domains

Professional and Enterprise plan subscribers can serve the platform from a custom domain.

To set up a custom domain, go to Settings > Workspace > Custom Domain. Enter your desired domain such as app.yourcompany.com and follow the DNS configuration instructions shown.

You will need to add a CNAME record pointing your domain to our servers and a TXT record to verify ownership. DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate.

SSL certificates are provisioned automatically via Let's Encrypt once DNS verification is complete. Certificate renewal is handled automatically.

Custom domains are workspace-scoped. Each workspace can have one primary domain and up to three additional aliases.
