Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all new subscriptions. If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact support for a full refund. No questions asked.

After the 30-day window, refunds are issued at our discretion for documented technical issues that prevented normal use of the platform. Usage-based charges are non-refundable.

Annual plan refunds after the 30-day window are prorated to the number of unused full months remaining. Partial months are not refunded.

To request a refund, open a support ticket with the subject "Refund Request" and include your account email and the reason for the request. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.

Accounts that have violated the terms of service are not eligible for refunds.
