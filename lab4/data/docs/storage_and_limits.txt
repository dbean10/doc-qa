Storage Limits and Quotas

Storage limits depend on your plan tier.

Free plan: 1 GB storage, 3 projects, 5 team members.
Starter plan: 10 GB storage, unlimited projects, 15 team members.
Professional plan: 100 GB storage, unlimited projects, unlimited team members.
Enterprise plan: Custom storage, unlimited everything, dedicated support.

Storage usage is calculated as the sum of all files uploaded to your workspace including attachments, images, and exported data. Database records do not count toward storage.

When you approach your storage limit, you will receive an email notification at 80% and 95% usage. Uploading new files is blocked when you reach 100%.

To free up storage, delete unused files from the Files section of your workspace. Deleted files move to Trash and are permanently removed after 30 days.
