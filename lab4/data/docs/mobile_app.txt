Mobile App

The platform is available as a native app for iOS and Android.

Download the iOS app from the App Store and the Android app from Google Play. Search for the platform name to find the official app.

Log in with your existing account credentials. If your workspace uses SSO, tap "Sign in with SSO" and enter your workspace domain.

The mobile app supports offline access to recently viewed content. Changes made offline sync automatically when you reconnect.

Push notifications can be configured in the app under Settings > Notifications. You can also manage them from your device's system notification settings.

Biometric authentication including Face ID, Touch ID, and fingerprint can be enabled from the app's Settings > Security menu for faster and more secure login.
