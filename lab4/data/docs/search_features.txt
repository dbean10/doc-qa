Search Features

The platform includes full-text search across all content in your workspace.

To search, click the search bar at the top of any page or press Cmd+K on Mac or Ctrl+K on Windows and Linux. Type your query and press Enter.

Search results are ranked by relevance. Use quotes to search for an exact phrase: "quarterly report" will only match that exact sequence of words.

Filters narrow your results by content type, date range, author, or project. Click "Filters" next to the search bar to access them.

Saved searches let you quickly repeat frequent searches. After running a search, click "Save this search" and give it a name. Saved searches appear in the left sidebar.

Search indexes are updated in near real-time. New content typically appears in search results within 60 seconds of being created.
