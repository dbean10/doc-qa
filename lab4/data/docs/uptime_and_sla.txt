Uptime and SLA

We target 99.9% uptime for all paid plans. This corresponds to a maximum of approximately 8.7 hours of downtime per year.

Current and historical uptime data is published on our status page at status.example.com. You can subscribe to incident notifications via email or RSS from that page.

Scheduled maintenance windows occur on the first Sunday of each month between 02:00 and 04:00 UTC. These windows are announced at least 72 hours in advance on the status page.

Enterprise plans include a financially-backed SLA with service credits if uptime falls below the guaranteed threshold. Contact sales for details.

In the event of an incident our team posts updates to the status page within 30 minutes of detection and continues posting updates at 30-minute intervals until resolved.
