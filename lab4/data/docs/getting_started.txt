Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the platform. This guide covers the first steps to set up your account and begin using the core features.

To create an account, visit the signup page and enter your email address and a password. You will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes. Click the confirmation link to activate your account.

Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard. The dashboard displays your recent activity, quick-access shortcuts, and system notifications. You can customise the layout by dragging panels to new positions.

Your first step after login should be to complete your profile. Navigate to Settings > Profile and fill in your name, timezone, and notification preferences. Completing your profile unlocks all platform features.

If you do not receive a confirmation email, check your spam folder. You can also request a new confirmation email from the login page by clicking "Resend confirmation."
